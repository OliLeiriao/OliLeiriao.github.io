"Integrated Circuit" (2022)

This piece, by artist Hiraoki Kamei, was on display at the Tokyo Metropolitan Art Museum when I visited in April 2024 (photographed by myself).
The piece combines an impressive array of materials, including a CPU, solder & metallic acrylics on layered wood pannels.
I enjoy how the piece engages with the ideas of abstraction, and of infinite possibility within the limits of finite computational capacity.
