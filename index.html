"Integrated Circuit" (2022)

This piece, by artist Hiraoki Kamei, was on display at the Tokyo Metropolitan Museum of Art when I visited in April 2024.
The piece combines an impressive array of materials, including a actual CPU, solder, metallic acrylics, and layered wood sheets.
I enjoy how the piece engages with the ideas of abstraction, and of infinite possibility within the limits of finite computational capacity.
